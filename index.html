FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What CAD software do you use?

Software is selected around the project requirements and required file formats. Common neutral formats such as STEP, STL and PDF drawings can be supplied.

Can you work from sketches or photographs?

Yes. Initial concepts can be developed from sketches, photographs, measurements, existing parts or written requirements.

Can you sign an NDA?

Yes. Confidential projects can be discussed under a non-disclosure agreement.

Do you offer fixed-price quotations?

Yes. Where the scope is clear, work can be quoted at a fixed price before the project begins.

Can you help manufacture the design?

Yes. Support can include supplier-ready files, design revisions, quotation support and communication with manufacturers.

Do you offer reverse engineering?

Yes. Existing components can be recreated or improved using measurements, samples and available drawings.

Can you produce prototypes?

Yes. Prototype options can include 3D printed parts and support with other manufacturing routes.

How quickly can you start?

Availability depends on the project size, but urgent and smaller tasks can often be reviewed quickly.

Do you work with individuals as well as businesses?

Yes. JP Innovation supports businesses, manufacturers, start-ups, inventors and individuals.

Can you visit a customer site?

Yes. Site visits can be arranged where measurements, equipment access or real-world assessment is useful.
