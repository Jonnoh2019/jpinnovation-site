Common Questions

Clear answers about access, pricing and privacy.

Can anyone enter the Innovation Hub?

No. Innovation Hub access is currently limited to approved trial accounts while the platform is tested privately. Free customer project access is handled separately through the Client Portal.

Will I be charged when I register interest?

No. Registering interest does not create an account or take payment. Pricing will be confirmed before Innovation Hub membership opens.

How will the Hub be moderated?

Innovation Hub access will be approved, posting rules will be enforced and professional profiles can be reviewed before verification.

Are private quote prices hidden from other engineers?

Yes. The customer will see submitted quotations; engineers will not see competing providers' prices.
